Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jövö India?

Jövö India is a premium lighting and automation solutions provider based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. With over 12+ years of expertise, we specialize in custom and architectural lighting, home automation, AV (Audio-Visual) solutions, and security systems. We take pride in being India’s first lighting company to offer upto 7-year warranty on our products, setting a benchmark in durability and customer satisfaction.

What services does Jövö India offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services that cater to both residential and commercial spaces:

  • Custom & architectural lighting
  • Home automation systems
  • AV (Audio-Visual) integration
  • Security & surveillance systems
  • Lamps, shades, and premium lighting accessories

What makes Jövö India different from other lighting companies?

At Jövö India, we stand out with an unmatched offer upto 7-year warranty—making us the first and only lighting company in India to offer such long-term reliability. Backed by over two decades of expertise in lighting, automation, and security, we provide complete end-to-end solutions from consultation to installation and after-sales support.

What does the offer upto 7-year warranty cover?

Our industry-leading offer upto 7-year warranty covers manufacturing defects, product performance issues, and technical malfunctions. This warranty applies to our specific lighting products and ensures you enjoy peace of mind with every purchase. Terms and conditions apply, and our team will be happy to guide you through the specifics based on your project and products.

Do you offer services outside Bengaluru?

Yes, while we are headquartered in Bengaluru, our services extend pan India. We work with clients nationwide, regardless of location or project scale. Feel free to reach out to discuss your specific requirements and how we can support your project anywhere in the country.

High Quality, bespoke lighting solutions sold. we sell solutions than products. complete lighting for your space.
